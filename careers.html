CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

ITC is a Johnson Controls Authorized Building Control Specialist (A.B.C.S.) serving the greater New York area. We are innovative and are looking to set the difference in a rapidly growing industry with energy conservation solutions for tomorrow.

We are currently considering applicants for the following positions:

ENGINEERING

Field Engineer: An entry level position that requires a mechanically inclined person with understanding of control wiring, Visio or Autocad. In this position you will be trained to prepare submittals and assist in the commissioning of projects. Traveling in the area to job sites is required.

Application Engineer: Experienced person preferably with Johnson Control knowledge capable of taking a job from design to completion, including preparation of submittals, ordering material, programming and supervise commission of projects. Traveling in the area to job sites is required.

FIELD TECHNICIANS

Technician: Experienced field technician preferably with Johnson Control knowledge. HVAC experience is required with understanding of control wiring diagrams.
