OUR SERVICES

Although we started as a service-based company, ITC has grown to become a full-service contracting firm, including sales, service and engineering. Offering many years of experience in the automatic temperature control industry, ITC’s skilled professionals take great pride in their workmanship. The company provides annual service agreements and maintenance contracts to meet the unique needs of each client. Additionally, ITC’s service department consists of experienced technicians who can cover all the temperature control needs for pneumatic, electric and digital controls. All of ITC’s technicians have qualified factory training on major manufacturer control systems and are on call 24 hours a day for emergency service.

Servicing All Building Sectors

ITC serves a wide variety of clients in practically every industry imaginable including hospitals, hotels, movie theaters, water treatment plants, office buildings, schools, college campuses, commercial high-rise office buildings, laboratory environments and many more.

One prominent client ITC serves is the New York City School Construction Authority (NYCSCA). For the last decade, ITC has worked on more than 100 NYCSCA projects, serving as either the automatic temperature control contractor, mechanical contractor or the general contractor.

A Revolutionary Energy-Saving Discovery

In 2006, ITC was awarded a $28.3 million controls contract with the 86-acre, 110 apartment complex, Peter Cooper Village in NYC, one of the largest contracts in the temperature controls industry’s history. As ITC worked on the project, it made a landmark discovery — by modulating steam supply temperature, they could conserve energy, save the client millions of dollars on their fuel consumption bill, and maintain more consistent temperature levels for the apartment residents. Because of this innovation, the project will pay for itself in four to five years. ITC is in the process of securing a global patent for this innovative discovery.
